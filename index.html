Story
The medieval of the town of Lancaster: a peaceful town made up of 8 powerful guilds, each with their own ability. The guilds were once united - 40 years ago during the Battle of the Roses, in which the Hero of Lancaster, who brought together all 8 guilds through the mastery of their abilities defeated the evil White Rose Clan. But since then, the guilds have slowly started to dissipate.

Nowadays, life is much more simple. No longer about defending Lancaster, the guilds have slowly adapted their ways to be more peaceful.

However, this all changed when a young talent from the Cartmel Guild noticed a change in the landscape over the Great Lonsdale Wall, they have come to the realisation that the great White Rose Clan has come to claim what they tried to capture 40 years ago.

Now, you the player must unite all the guilds of Lancaster, earn their power and challenge the invaders.






Controls and Actions

Movement controls:

  • Use arrow keys ← → or A D to move left and right respectively.
  • Press ↑, Space or W to jump.

Abilities:

  • Use J or 1 to activate the ability in the first slot.
  • Use K or 2 to activate the ability in the second slot.
  • Use L or 3 to activate the ability in the third slot.
Navigating the Interface

Main Menu:

  • Press New Game to start a new game. This will delete and previous saves.
  • Press Continue Game to continue a saved game.
  • Press Credits to view the credits.
  • Press Exit Game to exit the game.

Level Select Screen:

  • Use the ← → keys or A D keys to navigate through the map.
  • Upon selecting the map that you intend to play, press Enter.

Ability Select Screen:

  • Before the level starts, you will gain a preview of what lies ahead.
  • With that information in mind, you will be prompted to choose your abilities.
  • Use the ← → keys or A D keys to cycle through the abilities.
  • Upon highlighting the ability of your choice, press Enter to put the ability in your slot.
  • Alternatively, you may press the slot button (1, 2, 3 or J, K, L) to manually insert a ability into that specified slot.

Pause Menu:

  • Press Escape in game or during the Level Select Screen to pause the game.
  • Select the option of your choice and press Enter.
  • Options will differ depending if you're in the Level Select Screen or whether you are in game.

Enemies

Grunt Goose
Most common and basic Infantry in the armies of White Rose Clan. Whilst rather stupid, these grunts damage the player when they come into contact, although they are susceptible to being damaged from above.

Goose Spitter
The deadly Spitter of the White Rose army are a ranged unit that shoot a toxic ball of spit across the screen. If the player is hit by one of these projectiles they take damage.

Spiked Goose
A higher ranked Grunt Goose, these geese have a helmet to show their rank, which is coated in an array of deadly spikes that'll be a challenge to get around.

Spikes
An enemy that doesn't move, coming into contact with spikes will deal damage the player.

Abilities

Bow of Bowland
The Bow of Bowland shoots an arrow horizontally across the screen damaging any enemy that may be in the way. Face the direction in which you want to shoot, and press the activate key.

Wings of Cartmel
Use the Wings of Cartmel by jumping from a height and glide back down to the ground by holding down the activate key

Calm of County
You become one with nature and forget all of life's troubles, everything around you is temporarily slowed down once the activate key is pressed.

Furness Footwear
Allows you to jump an extra time immediately after jumping the first time, this process allows the player to reach higher heights, to areas which may not normally be accessible to them. Press the active key immediately after jumping to reach extra height.

Fylde Windwalker
Gives the player the speed of the wind, allowing them to dash forward a short distance, upon pressing the activation key.

Grizedale Concoction
Upon drinking the Grizedale concoction, the player becomes more hardy and resistant to damage for a short period of time. The ability is active as soon as the key is pressed.

Lonsdale Roar
Unleash the roar of the Lonsdale Lion to terrify nearby enemies freezing them in place. The ability is active as soon as the key is pressed.

Pendle Potion
Activating the County Concoction hurls a flask of poison that smashes on contact with a surface or entity. Upon smashing, the concoction spills a deadly poison across the floor which disables any enemy that touches it.

Damage and Loss of Life
  • The player starts with 1 health point.
  • If the player is damaged by an enemy, they will lose a health point.
  • If the player's health point goes below 1, they will die and will be moved to the start of the screen.
  • Some abilities may allow the player to take extra damage.
  • Some levels can be hard. If you are having trouble finishing a level, feel free to take a break and return to it at another time.
Finishing a Level
  • Levels are composed of multiple screens, complete each screen in order to finish the level. For most levels, there are three screens.
  • Screens are completed by moving from the left hand side to the right, this is the same for levels.
  • After finishing the last screen of the level the player is presented with the "completed level" screen which gives you the completion time of the level.
  • If a level has not been completed yet, you will need to use the corresponding ability within that level.
  • If a level is completed has not been completed before, you will earn the new ability, which can be used from that point in the game.
